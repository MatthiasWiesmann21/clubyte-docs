# Course Interaction

This guide explains how Users go through courses and what features were available on the Clubyte Platform. These courses will be displayed on the Course Page when published.

### Watching the Video

Users who have access to a chapter (whether paid or free) can watch the video provided in the chapter. This is the primary mode of content delivery.

### Liking the Chapter

Users can like a chapter by clicking the "Like" button. This feature helps indicate which chapters are particularly appreciated by the audience.

### Marking as Complete

Users can mark a chapter as complete once they finish it. This helps them keep track of their progress and encourages a sense of accomplishment.

### Writing a Comment

Users can leave comments on a chapter, providing feedback or engaging in discussions about the course content. This interaction can help improve the course and foster a community of learners.

### Navigating Through the Course

Users can navigate through different chapters of the course using the course navigation tools. This allows them to follow the course structure and progress through the material systematically.
