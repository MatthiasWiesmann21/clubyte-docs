# Managing Your Profile on Clubyte Community Pages

This guide explains how to manage your user profile in the current Clubyte Container from your Creator/Association. You can change your username, manage your account settings, and update your profile picture easily through your profile page.

## Managing Your Account

To manage your account settings, including changing your password, deleting your account, and updating your profile picture, follow these steps:

1. **Click on your username** next to your profile image.
2. **Select "Manage Account"** from the dropdown menu.

## Changing Your Username

To change your username, follow these steps:

1. **Log in** to your account.
2. **Click on Manage Account** located next to your profile image at the top-right corner of the page.
3. Edit **Username** in the provided field and save the changes.

![Changing Usernam](/changing-Username.png)

### Changing Your Password

1. In the **Manage Account** section, navigate to the "Change Password" tab.
2. Enter your current password, then your new password, and confirm the new password.
3. Click **"Save"** to update your password.

![Changing Password](/Changing-Password.png)

### Changing Your Profile Picture

1. In the **Manage Account** section, navigate to the "Profile Picture" tab.
2. Click on **"Change Profile Picture"**.
3. Upload a new image from your device.
4. Adjust the image if necessary and click **"Save"**.

### Deleting Your Account

1. In the **Manage Account** section, navigate to the "Delete Account" tab.
2. Read the instructions and confirm the action.
3. Click **"Delete Account"** to permanently delete your account.

![Deleting Profie](/delete-profile.png)

By following these steps, you can easily manage your user profile on the Clubyte Community Pages. If you encounter any issues or have further questions, please contact support at [support@clubyte.com](mailto:support@clubyte.com).
