# Authentication on Clubyte Community Pages

Welcome to the guide on how to authenticate on Clubyte Based Community Platforms. This documentation will walk you through the steps to sign up and verify your account.

## Step 1: Navigate to the Login Page

Open your preferred web browser and enter your community Url (e.g. app.clubyte.live). You can find it at [https://app.clubyte.live](https://app.clubyte.live).

![Test](/Green-Check-Mark.png) 

## Step 2: Sign In or Register

If you already have an account, enter your login credentials (email address and password) and click "Continue". If you don't have an account yet, click "Register" and follow the instructions to create an account.

## Step 3: Email Verification

After registering, you will receive a confirmation email. Open this email and type in the generate verification code to verify your E-Mail Adress to your Account. If you can't find the email, please check your spam folder.


## Step 4: Complete the Sign-In

After verification, you can sign in to the Clubyte Community page with your login credentials. Enter your email address and password, then click "Continue".


## Common Issues and Solutions

### Forgot Password

If you forgot your password, click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page. You first have to type in your E-Mail to see the forgot password link above the password input field. Enter your email address, and you will receive an email with instructions to reset your password.


### Did Not Receive Email

If you did not receive the confirmation email, check your spam folder or request a new confirmation email from the login page.

## Signed-Up but didn't get in the application

If you correctly sign-up the first time and your landing on a blank page, please refresh/reload the page. This should reload the functions and you have then access to your Community Favorite Clubyte Container. This Issue only appears on first sign-up.



With this guide, you should be able to authenticate on the Clubyte Community Pages without any issues. If you have further questions, please contact support at [support@clubyte.live](mailto:support@clubyte.live).
